Makui

Quiosque

MAKUI is conceived as a compact yet expressive retail structure — a quiosque that operates as both product display and brand statement.

The concept explores contrast between structure and lightness, creating a space that feels open, inviting, and visually distinctive within a commercial environment.

Through careful spatial organization and mood development, the kiosk enhances visibility, flow, and interaction — turning a small footprint into a strong experiential presence.

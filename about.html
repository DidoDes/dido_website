Hi, I'm André Ostheimer

I'm a graphic / motion designer based in São Paulo, Brazil. I specialize in creating dynamic visual systems, interactive experiences, and real-time graphics.

My work sits at the intersection of design and technology, combining branding, motion design, and creative coding to create engaging digital experiences.

With a background in graphic design and a passion for experimental visuals, I explore the possibilities of generative art, interactive installations, and motion systems that respond to user input.

What I Do

Motion Design

Creating dynamic brand identities and motion systems that bring visual concepts to life.

Creative Coding

Building interactive experiences using p5.js, Processing, and other creative tools.

Real-Time Graphics

Developing generative visuals and interactive installations for live performances.

Branding

Designing cohesive visual identities that work across digital and physical spaces.

Web Design

Creating engaging digital experiences with modern web technologies.

Video Production

Producing compelling motion graphics and video content for various platforms.
